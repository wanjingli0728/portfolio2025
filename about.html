Wanjing Li, a recent BU Graphic Design BFA graduate, is a graphic designer and artist based in Boston and New York City.
She specializes in web design, experience design, space installations, and typefaces. Her work explores graphic form for cultural translation, aiming to create shared spaces that promote social good, culture, and education. While her range of work spans across various mediums, she is always looking for new ways to shape ideas through design.
