Wanjing specializes in digital experiences, brand identity and motion graphics. Her work explores graphic form for cultural translation, aiming to create shared spaces that promote social good, culture, and education. While her range of work spans across various mediums, she is always looking for new ways to shape ideas through design.
She is currently developing AI-powered emotional companion experiences for children at TETRABOT, merging interactive storytelling with gamified learning.
